From waste to value

A circular approach to plastics

Since 2003, Total Recycling has been reprocessing plastic waste into engineered compounds that give discarded material a second life — without asking our customers to compromise on consistency or performance.

Recover

We source post-consumer and post-industrial plastic streams and sort them with advanced filtration technology.

Reprocess

Our lab-controlled compounding lines transform raw waste into consistent, standards-compliant PP, PE and PS pellets.

Reinvent

Custom-engineered to each customer's specification, our compounds power new products across industry.

Quality & testing

Consistency, verified in-house

Every batch that leaves our facility is checked against strict, repeatable standards — so the compound you order today performs exactly like the one you order next year.

  • DSC analysis Differential Scanning Calorimetry to verify thermal behaviour and material purity.
  • Melt Flow Index testing ISO 1133 melt flow testing on every grade, at the temperatures and loads each polymer requires.
  • High-temperature furnace Ash-content analysis at up to 1000°C to confirm filler ratios on talc-filled grades.

Sustainability

From waste to value

We convert PCR (post-consumer recycled) plastic waste into high-performance, custom-engineered compounds — reducing waste and carbon footprint while giving plastic a second life.

ESG framework

Environmental, Social and Governance principles guide our strategy and long-term planning — shaping how we act responsibly toward the environment, our people and our partners.

PPWR compliance

We support the EU Packaging & Packaging Waste Regulation, which sets mandatory minimum recycled-content requirements for new plastic packaging placed on EU markets.

ELVR compliance

Our processes align with the End-of-Life Vehicles Regulation, aimed at preventing waste and increasing the reuse, recycling and recovery of materials.
